White semiprecious stones make affordable white jewelry: white necklaces, white earrings, white pendants and white bracelets.  White jewelry can be worn with white, black and any other dark colors. White can also look very nice with yellow.  White jewelry with pink dress is too passé.  That was the 60s.  Jewelry in white stones with orange is fashionably flashy.  You can look for jewelry in gem stones of: White Agate, moonstone, snow onyx, pearl or mother of pearl, dendrite opal, or just pure crystal (quartz or rutilated quartz).

 

White is a great color for winter where snow is part of the region.  White jewelry has been part of the human fashion business since the very first woman wore jewelry in pre-historic times.  White was the first color in jewelry to be adorned by any woman.  The reason being that earlier jewelry was made from bones and pearls and shells of pearls (mother of pearl).

 

Pure white jewelry can be distractive for some skin tones, so use of duller white semiprecious stones to make jewelry is recommended.  Like peach moonstone or dendrite opal.
