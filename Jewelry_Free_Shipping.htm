SHIPPING & RETURN POLICIES

We ship within 48 hrs by USPS (3 to 5 days from shipping). 2nd day orders are shipped within 24 hrs by USPS or DHL (overnight to 2 day). For Crhistmas, last day to order would be 22nd December with 2nd day option. For one item, the basic shipping charges is only $1.95 which is amongst the lowest. If you buy more than one item, we cut our profits to pay for part of our shipping, you pay only $0.50 and if you buy three or more we dont charge you the basic charges.

International Shipping: There is a reduced shipping charge for international orders  as well:

Canada: We charge $1 extra for the task of going to the post office for filling out the customs form. Rest is the same as the US.

Europe & Australia: $4 extra. For one items $1.95 + $4.00 = $5.95. For two items $4.00

Asia & Africa: $15 extra, minimum order is $30. $15 is to do registered mail, so the postal services there do not steal the items.
